About

Welcome to this blog about strategy games!

The mid-90s and the beginning of the 2000s is often referred to the golden age of strategy games. Multiple epic strategy games such as Age of Empires, Warcraft and Tropico were born during this period and they founded new franchises of strategy games that keeps going strong.

This blog reviews these games and their newer once development through their franchise. Enjoy!
